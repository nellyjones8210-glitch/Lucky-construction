About Lucky Construction

Lucky Construction specializes in building construction from foundation to roofing or finishing level, residential construction for both private and public sectors, commercial construction, and renovations of old buildings to modern standards.

We also deal in buying and selling properties like land, houses, and buildings. With over 10 years of experience, our team of professionals is dedicated to providing high-quality construction services to help our clients build their dreams and create functional, beautiful, and sustainable spaces.
